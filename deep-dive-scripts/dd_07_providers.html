What Providers Are

Providers are the vendors, suppliers, distributors, and subcontractors your business works with. In Rev.io PSA, a Provider record must exist before a Purchase Order can be created — every PO requires a linked Provider. Providers also serve as a reference point for subcontracted work on tickets.

→ Connects ToProviders are required on Purchase Orders. They can be linked to ticket notes and comments as a reference when work is subcontracted. Provider records store the contact and account information needed for procurement without leaving the PSA system.

3
Subcontractors as Providers

If you subcontract work to external technicians or companies, those subcontractors can be set up as Providers. This lets you reference them on tickets, create POs for subcontracted labor, and track subcontractor spend in reporting.

For subcontractors, the 'Notes' field is especially important — capture their rates, skills, territory, certification status, and availability notes.

❓ PromptDo you use subcontractors or third-party technicians for any type of work? If so, let's add them as providers so they can be tracked in the system.

Action Items

  • Add all regular parts and equipment vendors
  • Add all subcontractors and third-party technicians
  • Enter account numbers for all vendors you have accounts with
  • Document lead times and special ordering notes in the Notes field
  • Deactivate any vendors you no longer use
