1
What Contracts Manage and Why They Matter

A Contract in Rev.io PSA is the formal record of a service agreement between you and a customer. It does three things automatically once active: it applies your contracted pricing rates to work done under the agreement, it generates recurring billing charges on schedule, and it tracks all work history so you always know what's been consumed against the contract.

Without contracts configured, your managed service customers get billed at standard catalog rates instead of their agreed-upon rates. Work that should be included gets billed. Work at a special contracted rate gets billed wrong. Contracts are the mechanism that makes your billing agreements enforceable automatically.

→ Connects ToContracts connect to: Customers (contracts live on a customer's Service tab), Product Catalog (contract rates reference catalog items), Billing (billing parameters generate recurring charges), Tickets (work is tracked against the contract, contracted rates applied), Time Logs (hours logged under the contract), Recurring Services (work together for full managed service billing), and Analytics (contract utilization and profitability reports).

Contract Rates — Complete Coverage

Contract Rates define the pricing rules applied when work is done under this contract. Navigate to the Contract Rates tab inside the contract.

For each type of work covered by the contract, add a rate entry:

FieldDescription
Catalog ItemThe product/service this rate applies to
Contracted RateThe price charged under this contract (may be lower or zero)
BillableWhether this item is billable or included at no charge
Not to ExceedMaximum billable amount for this item under the contract

Common Contract Rate Configurations

ScenarioRate Setup
Remote support includedRemote Support Labor item, Rate: $0, Billable: No
On-site at discountOn-Site Labor item, Rate: $95/hr instead of $125/hr standard, Billable: Yes
Block of hoursLabor items, Billable: Yes, draws down from prepaid hours bank
Parts at costParts items, Rate: Cost price instead of sell price, Billable: Yes
Everything coveredAll relevant items, Rate: $0, Billable: No — flat fee covers all
❓ PromptFor each service your team might do for this customer, what's the rate? Included, discounted, or standard? Let's map every type of work.
⚠ Watch OutIf you don't set up contract rates, PSA will bill at standard catalog prices for work done by techs — even when that work should be included or discounted. Contract rates must be explicitly configured.

Billing Parameters — Recurring Contract Charges

The Billing Parameters tab sets up any recurring fee associated with this contract. This is the flat monthly charge that generates automatically — your managed service monthly fee, your maintenance plan annual charge, etc.

FieldDescription
AmountThe recurring charge amount
FrequencyMonthly, Quarterly, Semi-Annual, Annual
Start DateWhen the recurring charge begins
Next Bill DateCalculated automatically — review to confirm
In Advance / In ArrearsWhether charge generates at start or end of period
Catalog ItemThe catalog item the charge references for description on invoices
TaxWhether this recurring charge is taxable
❓ PromptDoes this contract have a recurring flat fee? If yes: what's the amount, how often, and in advance or arrears?
5
Contract History Tabs
  • Documentation: Attach the signed agreement, SOW, or any supporting files directly to the contract.
  • Ticket History: All tickets worked under this contract with links to each.
  • Parts & Labor History: All parts used and labor performed under the contract — compare against what's included.
  • Time Logged: All time entries against tickets under this contract. Compare to included hours for block-hour contracts.
  • Billing History: All charges generated by this contract's billing parameters.
💡 Pro TipFor block-hour contracts, review the Time Logged tab monthly. If a customer is consistently using more hours than their block, have a proactive conversation about upgrading. If they're consistently under, they may be a candidate for a smaller plan.

Action Items

  • Create a contract for every active managed service, maintenance, or SLA customer
  • Build contract rates mapping every type of work: included, discounted, or standard
  • Configure billing parameters for any flat recurring fees
  • Set correct start and end dates for renewal tracking
  • Attach the signed agreement under the Documentation tab
  • Set up renewal alerts if auto-renew is not used
