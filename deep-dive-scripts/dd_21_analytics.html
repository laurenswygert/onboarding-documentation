Analytics — Your Business In Data

Every ticket your team closes, every hour they log, every invoice you send, every payment you receive — all of it flows into the Analytics module and becomes measurable, reportable data. Analytics is where you stop managing your business by feel and start managing it by fact.

Rev.io PSA's Analytics module includes a library of pre-built reports, a flexible custom report builder, configurable dashboards, and Revii — an AI-powered assistant for natural language data queries.

→ Connects ToAnalytics draws from every module in the platform: Service (ticket data), Time (time logs and timesheets), Billing (invoices, payments, charges, AR), Customers (account data), Inventory (parts usage and stock levels), Projects (project costs and timelines), Contracts (utilization and billing), and Recurring Services (recurring revenue).

Custom Report Builder

When pre-built reports don't answer the specific question you have, the custom report builder lets you construct exactly what you need.

Building a Custom Report

  • Choose your data source (Tickets, Time Logs, Billing, Customers, Inventory, etc.)
  • Select which fields to display as columns
  • Add filters to narrow the data (date range, customer, tech, status, etc.)
  • Choose a grouping dimension if aggregating (group by customer, by type, by tech)
  • Set sort order
  • Run the report — view in table or chart format
  • Save with a descriptive name — it appears in your report library

Saved custom reports can be run again anytime with the same configuration. You can also schedule them to be delivered by email on a recurring schedule.

❓ PromptIs there a specific business question you've wanted to answer that you couldn't with your current tools? Let's try to build that report together right now.
💡 Pro TipName custom reports descriptively: 'Monthly Revenue by Tech — Billable Only' not 'Custom Report 1'. You'll have many saved reports over time and descriptive names make them findable.

Dashboards — Your Business At a Glance

Dashboards pin your most important reports and metrics onto a single screen for at-a-glance business visibility. A well-designed dashboard tells you everything you need to know about your business health in 30 seconds.

Creating a Dashboard

Navigate to Analytics > Dashboards > Add Dashboard. Give it a name, then add widgets. Each widget is a report or a key metric displayed as a number, chart, or table. Arrange widgets by drag-and-drop.

Revii — AI-Powered Analytics

Revii is Rev.io PSA's AI analytics assistant. Instead of building a report, you ask a question in plain English and Revii answers with data from your PSA.

Example Queries

  • 'Which technician closed the most tickets last month?'
  • 'Show me revenue by customer for Q3'
  • 'What are my 10 largest outstanding invoices?'
  • 'How many hours did we log on-site vs. remote this quarter?'
  • 'Which customers haven't had a ticket in the last 90 days?'

Revii understands the context of your PSA data and returns answers based on your actual records. It's the fastest way to answer ad hoc business questions without building a formal report.

💡 Pro TipShow Revii to your operations manager or owner first — this is the feature that most impresses people who aren't living in the system daily. The ability to ask a question and get a data-backed answer instantly changes how people interact with their business data.
6
Report Scheduling and Delivery

Key reports can be scheduled to deliver automatically to specific email addresses on a recurring schedule. This is how you get data to stakeholders who need information from PSA but don't log into it regularly — owners, finance teams, board members, or customers.

Configure scheduled reports from the saved report's settings. Set the frequency (daily, weekly, monthly), the delivery date/time, and the recipient email addresses. The report runs automatically and is delivered as a PDF or CSV attachment.

💡 Pro TipSet up a monthly revenue report to deliver to your owner and a weekly AR aging report to deliver to your billing team. These two scheduled reports alone eliminate 90% of the manual reporting requests most finance teams deal with.

Action Items

  • Run the Ticket Summary and Revenue by Customer reports to establish baseline metrics
  • Build at least one custom report for your most important business question
  • Create an Operations dashboard and a Finance dashboard
  • Test Revii with 3–5 questions specific to your business
  • Set up a weekly AR Aging scheduled report for your billing team
  • Set up a monthly Revenue Summary scheduled report for your owner/management
